About Me

Dedicated, Educated & Responsible

My name is Margaret Wright, and I want your vote for the Kyrene School District Governing Board this November. I have lived in the Kyrene School District for nine years and my three children (ages 13, 11 and 8) have greatly benefited from the outstanding educators and programs offered through Kyrene. I have a deep desire to give back to this community. I have spent extensive time volunteering in classrooms and serving with the PTO as a board member, committee chair, and PTO president. I am an educator by profession. I received my BS degree in Zoology and graduated Cum Laude from Brigham Young University followed by a master’s degree in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology from the University of Colorado Boulder. I currently teach Biology as an Adjunct Professor at Paradise Valley Community College. I believe educating our children is the most important way to shape our society and I want to make a difference in the educational lives of all the children in our school district.

Why I'm Running

I want to make a difference in the educational lives of our students. I understand the importance education can make in determining one’s livelihood and ability to thrive. My husband Whitney, an entrepreneur orthodontist, and I have seen firsthand how education can play a critical role in success in one’s career and one’s ability to give back to the community. The company he co-founded, Risas Dental and Braces, has given more than $6 Million in free dental care to underserved communities since opening its doors in 2011. Witnessing firsthand how a little extra effort in helping someone along the way has shown me the importance to help as many children as possible to achieve greatness. Ultimately, I am a mother in the community with a vested interest in the success of the Kyrene School District. I am kind, considerate and able to make intelligent decisions with facts and data as my guide. I am interested in helping all parties come together for the well-being of our children, and their bright futures.


Please vote for Margaret Wright for Kyrene School Board Member. I believe in responsible governance, financial accountability, and civil conversation to achieve effective, efficient, and moderate solutions. I strive to understand the different perspectives from all community members and will work closely with the parents and educational administrators, teachers and staff to ensure I am doing my best to protect children and enhance our schools.

My Ideals

The children of the Kyrene District are my reason for serving on the governing board. Here are my ideals that will guide my decision making.

Student Wellness

  • a. Social and Emotional Health: Student Counselors at all 25 schools to ensure that our children feel supported, listened to and cared for.
  • b. Student Safety: Children need to feel safe in order to learn and achieve at the highest levels.
  • c. High quality talent: Recruit and retain extremely desirable administrators, teachers and support professionals.

Data Driven Decisions

  • a. Equity: All students achieving at their highest level.
  • b. Achievement Gap: Students of different ethnicities and socioeconomic backgrounds achieving at highest levels.
  • c. The Strategic Plan: Fully support the current strategic plan for the district.

Financial Accountability

  • a. Budget: Evaluate the data to determine where money would be best spent.
  • b. Tax Dollars: Understanding that each dollar spent comes from our community and needs to be spent wisely.
  • c. Property Values: High achieving schools increase property values for the homes around them. This is a way to give back to the community who supports us.

Every Voice Matters

  • a. Collaboration: Administrators, Educators, Parents and Students each have a voice in the matters pertaining to the district.
  • b. Civility: Treating all voices with kindness and respect.
  • c. Patience: Change takes time.
